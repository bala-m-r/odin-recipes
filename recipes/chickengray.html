Chicken Gravy

This a South Indian style dish which has high protein and rich taste

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken Gravy Masala
  • Coriander leaves

Recipe

  1. Pour some oil in the pan
  2. Put finely chopped Onions into the oil and saute it
  3. Put finely chopped Tomatoes in the pan and saute it too
  4. After that put the Chicken into the pan and close it for 10 minutes
  5. Put the Chicken Gravy Masala after the Chicken baked
  6. Put some Coriander leaves and Serve Chicken Gravy
