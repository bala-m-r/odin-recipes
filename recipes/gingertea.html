Ginger Tea

Ginger Tea is an Indian special tea which is sever as a medicine for lots of minor disease and it is very tasty

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Put the crushed Ginger into the boiling water
  2. Put some Tea powder
  3. Add some Milk
  4. Add little amount of sugar
  5. Serve as Hot
